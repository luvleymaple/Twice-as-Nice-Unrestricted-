Introduction To Twice as Nice!

At Twice as Nice, we believe that food is far too valuable to be wasted. Our Twice as Nice platform is dedicated to helping households in Myanmar rethink the way they manage leftovers. We aim to make sustainability simple, fun, and delicious through practical tools and creative ideas.

Whether you're a busy student, a working parent, or just someone trying to be more mindful, our mission is to support you in turning yesterday’s extras into today’s inspiration. By combining local insights with accessible tips, we hope to spark small changes that make a big difference both at home and in our communities.

Creative Cooking

We will help you turn yesterday’s meals into today’s magic.

Why We are Here

Our Mission

To turn leftovers into love! We’re here to help you reduce food waste by sharing creative, delicious leftover recipes and simple, sustainable tips. One dish at a time, we aim to make your kitchen more eco-friendly and your meals more meaningful.

What We Envision

Our Vision

To create a community where food is valued, not wasted.We imagine a future where everyone makes the most of their meals by saving leftovers, sharing recipes, and living more sustainably together.

What Drives Us

Our Purpose

We created this space for anyone who’s ever wondered, “What do I do with this leftover?”By offering simple, tasty recipes and eco-friendly advice, we want to inspire people community to cook with care, waste less, and live more sustainably.
