Anywhere, Anytime Recipe Cards

Download and cook your recipes wherever you are with no internet or anything. From quick bites to full meals, our downloadable and printable ccards will make your kitchen life easier!

Instructions

Step 1: Find Your Favorite Recipe

Scroll through the recipe cards and choose the one that looks the tastiest to you! Whether it’s creamy pasta or sweet rice pudding, pick the dish you love.

Step 2: Click on the Recipe Image

Once you’ve found your favorite recipe, click on the image to open it. This will make it larger and ready to save!

Step 3: Save and Download

Then select “Save image as…” to download it to your device. Now you’ve got it saved for easy access anytime!
