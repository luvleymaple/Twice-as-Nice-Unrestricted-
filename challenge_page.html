Cook, Share, Enjoy!

Weekly Leftover Cooking Challenge

"The best recipes come from daring to try. Step up to our cooking challenge and makeover your leftovers!"

How It Works
Check the Leftover of the Week
We feature one common leftover item your challenge ingredient! You can also browse our example dishes for ideas.
Cook it Up!
Grab that leftover and get cooking! Feel free to follow the examples or put your own spin on it.
Snap & Submit
Take a photo of your final dish and fill out our submission form. Outstanding creations will be featured on our website and will you be one of them...?
What you will gain
Get Featured & Recognized
Outstanding dishes will be proudly featured on our website, giving you a chance to be recognized and gain community love.
Spark Your Creativity
Think outside the (leftover) box. You will discover new ways to cook and innovate while sharpening your cooking skills.
Waste Less, Feel Good
By taking part, you’re not just cooking, you’re cutting down on leftover waste and doing something good for the planet.
